Deep Info Lab - "Enternship Program"

This is an opportunity hosted by us at RS Deep Info Lab Pvt. Ltd (known as Deep Info Lab) to build great engineers and leaders from the already skilled students of AIT. This will allow students to learn core technical skills and develop, while at the same time, learn how to handle a team in the future.

The work day will include development, accompanied by direct interaction with the CEO on their projects and weekly sit-ins on select corporate strategy meetings.


While not a requirement, students with any component out of the following skill sets will be preferred:
  • 1.Django
  • 2. ReactJS
  • 3. Tensorflow
  • 4. Keras
  • 5. OpenCV
  • 6. MySQL
  • 7. MongoDB

    • This event is exclusively for AIT students and we will be selecting 4 interns from the applicant pool. They will also have a chance of PPO, based on their performance with us..


    ✍️Please note that any student who is involved in the process but has no interest cannot be considered as there is strict entry.
