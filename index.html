E-Cell Problem statement

As shown in the image. The delivery agent is going to deliver one out of these 40 boxes to each customer. As all the boxes are similar in shape, size, colour. To identify the boxes as per the customer either QR Codes or BAR codes or Printout or holograms are attached which increase the operational cost. As it takes printing charges, paper charges, printers and PCs.
Find a solution which doesn't require any kind of sticker, paper, QR code to be attached on daily basis. Is it possible to stick a QR code\BAR Code\RFID tag permanently on the box and update it regularly or using an arrangement to remember the order of the boxes ??
So here we sought solutions from our First-year students. Try to include as much in-depth analysis as you could. The presentation may include the following but not limited to:

  • 1. Your solution
  • 2. How is it better than the conventional method.
  • 3. Cost breakup of your solution
  • 4. Can you make the prototype?

You can submit your solution in any form either a video of your own explaining the solution, a word file, a ppt or any other form.
The best solution may get a chance to work on the solution along with goodies.
Note: If you are planning for a transparent box as a solution then imagine all the customer ordered a mix of vegetables each containing the same 10 vegetables but quantity and quality is different then transparent one is not going to help. Consider a situation where all of them order a Nike shoe so all the boxes will look similar but inside they are of different size, colour. So please take in consideration of all the possible cases and solve after considering the privacy of the customer that you can't see what is inside these boxes your work as a delivery team is to deliver the boxes to respective customers safely and maintaining their privacy.
Feel free to contact if you have any query.
Mail your queries to aitecell@gmail.com or contact any coordinators from Ecell website.
